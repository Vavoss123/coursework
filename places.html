    Grangemouth & Falkirk

      The Kelpies

        The horses are named after the sinister creatures fabled in Scottish folklore, the Kelpies draw the attention from visitors all over. The humongous looming head of 2 horses bring a mythical and beautiful sensation that is unparralled.

      Jupiter Urban Wildlide Centre

        Jupiter was once a wasteland untill it was renivated into a ubran green space with a rich habitat for the wildlife and the centre is constantly changing. The staff are friendly and helpful and the activites are almost endless.

      Callendar Park

        Callender park has a amzing Roman Fort themed play area next a Natural Play trail leading to a half hour wander through the woods. Callendar Park and House are both connected but during my visit I only spent time at the park.
